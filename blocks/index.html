About Block Puzzle

Blocks is a classic block puzzle game that challenges you to fill rows and columns by dragging and dropping blocks onto the board. Clear completed rows and columns to earn points and keep the board from filling up. It is easy to learn but endlessly engaging, making it the perfect puzzle game for quick sessions or extended play.

Clear multiple rows or columns at once to earn combo bonuses and rack up high scores. Log in daily to collect rewards and unlock beautiful new color themes to customize your game board. With its clean interface and no pop-up ads, every session is pure, uninterrupted puzzle fun.
