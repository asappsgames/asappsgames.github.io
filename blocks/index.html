About Block Blast Puzzle Jewel

Blocks (also known as Block Blast Puzzle Jewel) is a free block puzzle game for iPhone and iPad with no pop-up ads, no interstitials, and no required in-app purchases. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Block Blast Puzzle Jewel free to play?

Yes. The game is free to download and play. There are no required in-app purchases — every core feature is unlocked from the start.

Are there pop-up ads in Blocks?

No. There are no pop-up ads, no full-screen interstitials, and no forced ad breaks during gameplay.

How do you score points in Blocks?

Drag and drop blocks onto the board to fill complete rows or columns, which then clear. Clearing multiple rows or columns in a single move triggers a combo bonus for extra points. Strikes (consecutive clears) award additional bonuses.

Does Blocks have daily rewards?

Yes. Logging in each day awards rewards that can be used to unlock new color themes and customize your board.

Does Blocks work offline?

Yes. The game works fully offline. No Wi-Fi or mobile data is required.

Is Blocks available on Android?
