About Color Blocks

Color Blocks is an addictive color-matching puzzle game where you drag and drop multi-colored blocks onto the board and match three or more blocks of the same color to clear them. As your score increases, new colors unlock, adding layers of strategy and challenge to every round.

Create combos by matching blocks vertically or horizontally for bonus points. Use boosters and the hammer tool strategically to overcome tough spots. With its progressive color-unlocking system, every game session feels fresh and rewarding.

Key Features

  • No pop-up ads during gameplay
  • Intuitive drag-and-drop controls
  • Match 3 or more same-color blocks to clear
  • Progressive color unlocking as you score higher
  • Combo bonus system for clearing multiple matches
  • Boosters and hammer tools for extra help
  • Game Center leaderboards and achievements
  • Beautiful, colorful visuals
  • Free to download and play
