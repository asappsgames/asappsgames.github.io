About Color Blocks

Color Blocks is a free color-matching block puzzle game for iPhone and iPad with no pop-up ads, no interstitials, and no required in-app purchases. Drag and drop multi-color blocks onto the board, match three or more blocks of the same color to clear them, create combos for bonus points, and unlock new colors as your score grows. Works fully offline.

As your score increases, new colors unlock, adding layers of strategy and challenge to every round. Use boosters and the hammer tool strategically to overcome tough spots. With its progressive color-unlocking system, every game session feels fresh and rewarding.
