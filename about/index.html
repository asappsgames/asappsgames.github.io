About ASApps Games

ASApps Games is a small, independent iOS studio making puzzle and card games that respect your time — no pop-up ads, no forced sign-ups, and no subscriptions just to play.

Why "no pop-up ads"

We make the kind of games we want to play ourselves — a quiet Sudoku before bed, a hand of Solitaire in a waiting room. The fastest way to ruin that is a full-screen ad dropping in the moment you finish a puzzle. So our games simply don't do that. There are no pop-up ads and no full-screen interruptions during play.

Being honest about it matters to us: our games are free, supported by a small banner and optional short ads you choose to watch — for example, to continue after a few mistakes or to get a hint. Nothing is forced, and every core feature is free.

How the games stay free — honestly

  • A small, unobtrusive banner — never a pop-up or a full-screen ad mid-game
  • Optional short ads you choose to watch (for a hint or an extra life) — never forced
  • An optional one-time Premium on some games — never a subscription
  • Every core feature is free, and every game works fully offline

Built for iPhone & iPad
