About Sudoku No Pop-up Ads

Sudoku No Pop-up Ads is a free Sudoku puzzle game for iPhone and iPad with no pop-up ads and no required in-app purchases. It works fully offline, ships with six difficulty levels — Beginner, Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert, and Master — and includes smart hints, fast notes, and unlimited undo.

Originally known as Number Place, Sudoku is the world's most popular logic-based number-placement puzzle. Fill the 9x9 grid so that each column, row, and 3x3 block contains every number from 1 to 9 exactly once.

Whether you are a complete beginner learning the basics or an expert looking for a serious challenge, the six difficulty levels are designed to progressively test your logical thinking and pattern recognition skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sudoku No Pop-up Ads really free?

Yes. The game is free to download and play. There are no required in-app purchases to access any of the six difficulty levels or core features.

Are there pop-up ads during gameplay?

No. There are no pop-up ads, and nothing forces an ad on you — the only ads are optional short clips you choose to watch (to continue after 3 mistakes or get a smart hint). That is the entire reason the app exists.

Does Sudoku No Pop-up Ads work without an internet connection?

Yes. The game is fully playable offline. No Wi-Fi or mobile data is required to start a new puzzle or continue an existing one.

What difficulty levels are available?

Six: Beginner, Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert, and Master. Each level uses different puzzle generation rules so the difficulty progression is consistent rather than random.

Does the game include hints?

Yes. Smart hints highlight the next logical step using actual Sudoku solving techniques rather than just revealing the answer, so they help you learn rather than skip the puzzle.

Is Sudoku No Pop-up Ads available on Android?

Currently iOS only — iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch. There is no Android version at this time.
