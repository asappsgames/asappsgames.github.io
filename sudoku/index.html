About Sudoku No Pop-up Ads

Sudoku No Pop-up Ads is a free Sudoku puzzle game for iPhone and iPad with no pop-up ads, no interstitial ads, and no required in-app purchases. It works fully offline, ships with four difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert — and includes smart hints, fast notes, and unlimited undo.

Originally known as Number Place, Sudoku is the world's most popular logic-based number-placement puzzle. Fill the 9x9 grid so that each column, row, and 3x3 block contains every number from 1 to 9 exactly once.

Whether you are a complete beginner learning the basics or an expert looking for a serious challenge, the four difficulty levels are designed to progressively test your logical thinking and pattern recognition skills.
