About Sudoku No Ads

Sudoku No Ads is a clean, beautifully designed Sudoku puzzle game for iPhone and iPad that lets you play without intrusive pop-up advertisements. Originally known as Number Place, Sudoku is the world's most popular logic-based number-placement puzzle. Fill the 9x9 grid so that each column, row, and 3x3 block contains every number from 1 to 9 exactly once.

Whether you are a complete beginner learning the basics or an expert looking for a serious challenge, Sudoku No Ads offers four carefully tuned difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert. Each level is designed to progressively test your logical thinking and pattern recognition skills.

Key Features

  • Four difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert
  • Smart hints to help you learn solving techniques
  • Fast Notes mode for quick pencil marks
