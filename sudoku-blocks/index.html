About Sudoku Blocks

Sudoku Blocks combines the strategic depth of Sudoku with the satisfying gameplay of block puzzles. Drag and drop blocks to fill rows, columns, and 3x3 squares on the board. When a row, column, or square is complete, it clears from the board and earns you points. The Sudoku-style grid adds a layer of spatial reasoning that sets this game apart from ordinary block puzzles.

Choose from over 20 unique block designs and two distinct game modes. Clear multiple lines or squares simultaneously to trigger combo bonuses and rack up high scores. Log in daily to earn rewards and unlock beautiful new color themes and visual customizations, including a dark mode for comfortable play at night.
