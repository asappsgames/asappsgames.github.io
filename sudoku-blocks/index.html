About Sudoku Blocks

Sudoku Blocks (Tetra) is a free puzzle game for iPhone and iPad that combines the strategic depth of Sudoku with the satisfying gameplay of block puzzles. No pop-up ads, no interstitials, and no required in-app purchases. Drag and drop blocks onto a 9x9 grid to fill rows, columns, and 3x3 squares — when one is complete, it clears and earns points. Includes 20+ block designs, dark mode, daily rewards, and a Block Pop mini game. Works offline.

The Sudoku-style grid adds a layer of spatial reasoning that sets this game apart from ordinary block puzzles. Choose from over 20 unique block designs and two distinct game modes. Clear multiple lines or squares simultaneously to trigger combo bonuses and rack up high scores. Log in daily to earn rewards and unlock beautiful new color themes and visual customizations.

Instead of placing numbers, you drag and drop block shapes onto a 9x9 grid to fill rows, columns, and 3x3 squares. It combines block puzzle gameplay with Sudoku's grid logic — there are no numbers involved.

Yes. The game is fully playable offline — no Wi-Fi or mobile data required.
