About Solitaire, FreeCell and Spider

Solitaire, FreeCell and Spider is a free Solitaire collection for iPhone and iPad — three classic card games in one app: Klondike, FreeCell, and Spider Solitaire. No pop-up ads, no full-screen interruptions, and no required in-app purchases (just an optional one-time upgrade, not a subscription) — and every game plays fully offline.

It's a calm, polished take on the classic solitaire card game: smooth animations, clean design, 13 background themes, and three ways to play every game — Classic deals, a worldwide Daily Challenge, or against the clock in Timed mode.

Klondike Solitaire — Draw 1 & Draw 3

Classic Klondike Solitaire is the version most people simply call "Solitaire" — the one that shipped with Windows for decades. Play it in both draw-one and draw-three modes (also called turn 1 and turn 3), with smart hints, unlimited undo, and auto-complete once the game is won. Pick a card face, a card back, and one of 13 background themes.

FreeCell Solitaire

FreeCell Solitaire is the strategy-first variant where nearly every deal is winnable — almost any game can be solved with the right moves, so it rewards planning over luck. (Some players type it as "free cell.") Free, fully offline, and with no pop-up ads on iPhone and iPad.

Spider Solitaire — 1, 2 & 4 Suit

Spider Solitaire comes in all three difficulties: 1 suit (the easiest, great for first-timers), 2 suit (the most popular everyday mode), and 4 suit (the full classic challenge) — just like the Spider Solitaire many people remember from Windows. Switch difficulty whenever you like, and play offline with no pop-up ads.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this include FreeCell and Spider Solitaire?

Yes. It's a 3-in-1 Solitaire collection: classic Klondike Solitaire (draw 1 and draw 3), FreeCell, and Spider Solitaire — all in one free app, all playable offline.

Which Spider Solitaire suit modes are available?

All three: 1 suit (the easiest, great for beginners), 2 suit (the most popular everyday mode), and 4 suit (the full classic challenge). You can switch difficulty any time.

Does Klondike Solitaire support draw 1 and draw 3?

Yes. You can play classic Klondike in both draw-one (turn 1) and draw-three (turn 3) modes — pick whichever you prefer before each game.

Is every FreeCell deal winnable?

Nearly all of them. FreeCell is famous for being almost always solvable — the vast majority of deals can be won with the right moves, which is what makes it so satisfying compared to Klondike.

Are there pop-up ads?

No. There are no pop-up ads and no full-screen interruptions. There's a small banner, and any short ad is optional — only when you choose. There are no required in-app purchases (just an optional one-time upgrade, not a subscription).

Does it work without Wi-Fi?

Yes. Klondike, FreeCell, and Spider all play fully offline — no Wi-Fi or mobile data is required.

Is Solitaire, FreeCell and Spider free to play?

Yes. The game is free to download and play, with no required in-app purchases.

Is it available on Android?

Currently iOS only — iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.
