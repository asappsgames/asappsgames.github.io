About Solitaire Classics

Solitaire Classics is a free classic Klondike Solitaire card game for iPhone and iPad with no pop-up ads, no interstitials, and no required in-app purchases. Smooth animations, both draw-one and draw-three modes, multiple card backs, and full offline play. Just pure, relaxing Solitaire whenever you need it.

Klondike — usually known simply as "Solitaire" because of the version that shipped with Windows for decades — remains the most-played card game on personal devices. This version focuses on getting out of your way: no pop-ups, no nag screens, no forced sign-ups.

Key Features
